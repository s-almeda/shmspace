CONTENT WARNING / TRIGGER WARNING; depression, suicidal ideation, self-harm 
(which, were all things I was really, really struggling with from 2016-2018)

this is probably the darkest and most vulnerable comic I've ever made and I get very scared about sharing it... but I hesitate to *not* share it: I feel like so many stories we end up hearing about suicidal people, are the ones where they don't make it...

if you haven't had a time in your life like this, you likely know someone who has / who is trying to live, in spite of this, right now. it sucks really really really bad. i found out that recovering does not mean that it stops sucking really bad. sometimes recovering sucks worse actually because it means you are doing the work. but i did. i kept getting help; i kept getting stronger; i started going to therapy and to several different psychiatrists, I took antidepressants (Lexapro -- escitalopram) for around 3-4 years, and while this helped, it wasn't perfect; i was dependent, and they changed the way I experienced my emotions. honestly they kinda just made me sleepy all the damn time but... you cannot unalive yourself if you are sleeping !!!!1 win!

in 2020 I starting working with my amazing wonderful life-changing current therapist Victor. when i was ready, he helped me start weaning myself off of anti-depressants... I now have access to *the full range of my often very extreme emotions* **and** the ability to have a functional, joyous, productive, healthy, disciplined life while feeling them! With the strength and space to nurture and care for others now, too! even when the world is very very bad and infuriating... living feels really beautiful! i'm so happy to be here!!

much of this is thanks to the privilege of having had access to so much support and community that I *could* reach out to. i want this for you, and for us all. 

i see you, you're not alone, never ever kys, etc., etc... please use the resources you have access to. reach out if you need help. ik it sucks. please do it anyway. thank you.
