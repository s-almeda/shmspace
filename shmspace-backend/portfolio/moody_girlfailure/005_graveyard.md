c. 2022
i do not remember what i was thinking when i made this (i was probably really high because i was constantly really high in this period of my life lol) 

but when i look at her i think she is asking you to indulge me: to mourn with me: to kneel at the grave of who i was: ...

i am constantly putting old versions of myself to rest and i guess that's what growing up is 

but hey-- is it this painful for you all, too? 

how are we not all grieving and screaming and holding each other's wracking bodies? 
how are we doing this? growing? becoming adults? unbecoming children? changing? 
how does anyone do this? 
how are we... paying parking tickets and moving across the country and donating clothes to goodwill and resetting our two-factor authentication and scrolling social media seeing journalists and children being murdered and knowing that i live in a world where great violence and injustice happens and has always happened and here we are resetting our two-factor authentication and cutting our bangs and opting-out of instagram using your data to train their AI and knowing this means nothing nothing nothing and i am so small and i feel myself jade

how are we not all constantly screaming together ?? clutching one another, doubled over in pain? how are we not?

is it that--- are you all doing this in private? alone?
how? 

I am so unbearably effusive and desperate for someone, anyone else to witness with me the death of my past selves and all of their dreams, who i was before i so intimiately knew all the ways i now know the world can be cruel, who i was before i became soooooo smart and powerful and wealthy and educated and cool, who i was before my hair turned green and my ribcage hardened to protect my heart in the places it had been worn down over and over again, callused, scabbing, who i was when i dreamed without constraint

over and over i grieve and i beg for someone to please, please hold my hand and watch my youth and innocence die with me. watch with me while the flames burn out. so I can feel like they were real. like they meant something

am I supposed to do be able to do this alone? because I live in america, where you are meant to be able to do everything alone? where to even ask for this form of attention feels so self-centered and needy and unproductive and frivolous and childlike...

but there is no way we are actually meant to be doing this alone, quietly, unwitnessed?

how is anyone doing this?

how is anyone doing this?
