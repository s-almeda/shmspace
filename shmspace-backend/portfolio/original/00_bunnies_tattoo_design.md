# My Rabbit Tattoo! 

This is the tattoo design that now lives on my left forearm, featuring my rabbits, Hopper (right) and Anya (left, with spots). I made this drawing; the design was adapted and actually tattooed by the great [Arlo Teague](https://www.magneticarts.tattoo/artists/arlo) at Berkeley's Magnetic Arts -- so grateful for their artistry and skill. They did such a wonderful job immortalizing my babies. The entire studio is awesome; I highly recommend it.

We ended up cutting out the drawings of the bunnies sitting, because my forearm didn't have enough circumfrence to fit all 5 drawings without making them absurdly small, lol. So we went with the kissing one right on my forearm, and the lounging about ones around the back of my arm.
